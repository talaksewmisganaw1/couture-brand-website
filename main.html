Our Story

Founded in 2010, Fert Design has grown from a small boutique in Addis Ababa to an internationally recognized couture house. Our founder's vision was to blend traditional Ethiopian craftsmanship with contemporary design aesthetics.

Each collection tells a story, drawing inspiration from Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage while embracing global fashion trends. We pride ourselves on sustainable practices and ethical production methods.

Our Atelier

Nestled in the heart of Addis Ababa, our atelier is where dreams become reality. Each piece is meticulously crafted by master artisans who have inherited centuries-old techniques, combined with modern innovation.

From the initial sketch to the final stitch, every creation is a testament to our commitment to excellence and the art of haute couture.
